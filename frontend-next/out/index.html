About Dr. Amruta Godbole

Toothfully speaking.

Dr. Amruta Godbole is a Mumbai-born, NYU-trained dentist with over two decades of clinical experience across two continents. She earned her DDS from NYU College of Dentistry in 2010, served as a teaching assistant, and completed a specialised aesthetics program before building a practice in New York and returning to Mumbai.

Her focus spans smile design, ceramic veneers, implants, and restorative care. She holds membership with the ADA, IDA, and Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry (an affiliate of the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry). At Toothfully Yours, patients feel this standard of care from their very first consultation.
